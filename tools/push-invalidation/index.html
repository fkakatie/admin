BYOCDN Push Invalidation Validator

The Push Invalidation Validator tool ensures your content delivery network (CDN) automatically purges outdated content on your production domain (e.g. www.yourdomain.com), whenever an author publishes changes. Use this tool to validate your project's vendor-specific properties and credentials (as found in the Excel workbook named .helix/config.xlsx in Sharepoint or the Google Sheet named .helix/config in Google Drive).
